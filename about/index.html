“I BELIEVE IN CREATING INSIGHTFUL DESIGNS, EXPERIENCES RICH IN STORY-TELLING, VISUALS WHICH ENTICE THE SPIRIT, STRATEGY WHICH CELEBRATES BUSINESS AND BRANDS WHICH INSPIRE PEOPLE.”
With over 300+ awards at the age of 10, Aakash’s journey in design didn’t start as expected.

A multidisciplinary artist since the tender age he had written plays, given state-level speeches and exhibited his works at various galleries.

His parents wanted him to pursue engineering but as fate would have it he was selected for an international camp when he had to appear for his engineering exams.

He followed his heart by joining the College of Art, Delhi. He bravely charted the unknown territory of his creative pursuits getting himself trained in advertising and graphic design.

With his college days pass by in researching about design thinking he ventured out in the world with a holistic view of design.

He was excited to see the power of design. How it connects every aspect of a business which lead to lovable companies.

A mix of marketing, strategy, story-telling and design he successfully branded TV shows, launched new platforms at Rider Mania for Royal Enfield and design campaigns for French Embassy.

With his roller coaster ride at tekie, he introduced himself to product design. An area where he could apply his analytical well as storytelling skills to build an app loved by parents, kids and adults alike.

Since then he has been designing experiences for UX in B2B and B2C industries and expanding his horizons in strategy.
